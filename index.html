To the only girl I've loved and will always love.

To the only girl I love, I've started and deleted this letter more times than I can keep track of. But no matter how I phrase it, it always comes down to me saying how much I love and cherish you. I know I've said this multiple times, but whenever you call yourself anything that isn't pretty, I just can't see how (?) or why, because every time I look at you, even when you just woke up or that time when you didn't have makeup, I always find you so pretty. I love the shape of your eyes, how soft your lips are, how soft your skin is, and how shy you get whenever you catch me staring at you. I also love the way you play with my hair because it makes me feel at ease. Whenever I think about you, all I can think about is how badly I want you and how much I want to take care of you. I want to be with you until we grow older. I want to be there when you achieve your dreams. I want to sleep next to you. I want to wake up next to you. I want to have matching keychains—or anything matchy—with you. I want to bake, cook, watch movies, travel, or do whatever comes to your mind as long as it's with you. You get the point—basically, I want to do everything with you.

But like every other relationship, I know we’re going to face a lot of challenges. We’ll have arguments. And if we do, I want us to talk about it and fix it every time. I don’t want you to hide anything from me, even if you think it’s not that big of a deal. I still want to know about it so I can understand you better. I know that sometimes you may not be able to communicate your feelings because you're shy or unsure, but don't worry, I get it, because I do too. But with that in mind, I'll try my best to understand you in any way that I can. And if I make a mistake, please tell me. Knowing all that, I'm still committed to what I've said earlier, and with all that I've poured into this letter, I just have one question. Will you be my girlfriend?
